About

Welcome to VYOMA, the aerospace extravaganza hosted by SJCITs's Aerospace Branch. Join us for a dynamic celebration of innovation and excellence in aerospace.

VYOMA is a melting pot where enthusiasts, students, and professionals converge to explore the boundless possibilities of flight. Dive into a weekend featuring cutting-edge technology, hands-on workshops, and thrilling competitions. Our festival is not just an event; it's a platform for networking, learning, and experiencing the future of aerospace. Curated by our dedicated team, VYOMA promises an unforgettable experience.
